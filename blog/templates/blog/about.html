Travis Brady is the founder of Be Inspired Coaching and the creator of The Next Generational Coach. He is a life transformation coach who mentors other
personal trainers and coaches in learning Next Generation transformational processes, helping them to build a 6-figure income each year.

He is the host of The 100k Coach’s Podcast & a board member of the National Speakers Association. Travis has been featured on every major television
station and numerous radio and podcast channels talking about Next Generation Coaching Processes that help leaders gain the skills to transform lives
faster. He speaks for major associations, businesses, seminars and host’s his own events.
